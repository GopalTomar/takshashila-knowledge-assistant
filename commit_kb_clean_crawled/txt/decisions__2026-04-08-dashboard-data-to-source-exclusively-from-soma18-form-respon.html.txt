[2026-04-08 11:07 UTC] @vanshikasaraf: Hello @all. Starting next week, all dashboard data will be sourced ***only*** from SOMA18 form responses. This means that any output not entered in the form will ***not*** be reflected in the dashboards.
Please note the following guidelines going forward:
1. Only include documents/op-eds/newsletters/blogs that have been ***published*** within the given week. Work in progress can be mentioned in the one-line update.
2. For co-authored outputs, please coordinate to ensure that only ***one author*** logs the entry. For instance, contributions to Technopolitik should not be recorded individually. The entry shall solely be made by the newsletter manager (in this case Anwesha). 
3. For ATPs, only the ***host*** of the episode should fill the entry.
4. For conference participations, ***do not*** count in-house events or those organised in partnership with Takshashila.
5. Please review your responses carefully before submitting the form. 
Thank you.
