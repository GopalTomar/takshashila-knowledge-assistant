✅ Zero-tolerance POSH policy; annual training mandatory for all staff

✅ Skill development reimbursement up to ₹10,000/year with manager approval
✅ Unreleased materials require explicit supervisor permission before external sharing
POSH Policy
All employees have the right to work in an environment free of sexual or other harassment. The policy applies beyond office hours to all work-related events including conferences and business trips. Consensual relationships between colleagues are not considered harassment under this policy.
Annual POSH training is mandatory
for all staff.
Leave & Work From Home
Consult the Leave Policy document (available on the Resources page of the staff handbook) before submitting any leave request.
Confidentiality
Unreleased materials — research drafts, internal documents, strategy papers — must not be shared externally without explicit permission from your supervisor. Employee contracts detail intellectual property classifications.
Skill Development
Takshashila reimburses up to
₹10,000 per year
for courses in soft skills, project management, multimedia editing, or languages. Conditions: - Manager approval required before enrolment - Assigned work must be completed before reimbursement is processed
Laptop Policy
Eligibility:
After completing the 6-month probationary period
Process:
1. Select your preferred device (Macs recommended) 2. Email your manager for approval, copying Shiva Kumar 3. Purchase the device 4. Submit receipt to Shiva Kumar 5. Reimbursement spreads across 24 months via payroll
Note:
One-time benefit. Reimbursement ceases if you leave the organisation.
