TL;DR
: Takshashila exists to advance freedom, openness, tolerance, pluralism, and responsible citizenship — and to strengthen India through better public policy and governance.
Mission
An independent centre for research and education in public policy. Non-partisan, non-profit. Dedicated to advancing
freedom, openness, tolerance, pluralism, and responsible citizenship
.
Drives change by uniting capable individuals with quality ideas and robust networks.
On Values (Nitin Pai)
Long-term continuity over growth-focused exits
Excellence is the primary observable measure of values
Diversity across multiple dimensions
Pro-market, not pro-business
Non-partisanship ≠ neutrality — policies and their consequences define values
Institutional survival is the biggest moral imperative
The lighthouse logo is the symbol: a beacon for these principles
Code of Conduct
Integrity
No plagiarism
Admit knowledge gaps openly
Disclose conflicts of interest
Maintain flexible hypotheses — update when evidence demands
Prioritise institutional goals and collaborative synergy over personal visibility
Conduct
Decency in all discourse
Disengage when discussions escalate unproductively
Mindful language and cultural sensitivity
Elevating Policy Debate
Recognise and credit original thought
Adopt interdisciplinary approaches
Judge policies by their human consequences, not political alignment
Independence
Maintain a clear personal-vs-institutional distinction; always use explicit disclaimers when expressing personal views
Prioritise India's national interests in framing
