Key Points
Low uptake observed for West Asia (Raja), China Defence (Anushka), and Geospatial (Nithiya) courses.
Hypothesis 1: Broader economic slowdown and hiring pauses are causing learners to deprioritize courses outside their immediate domain.
Hypothesis 2: Takshashila may be offering too many courses simultaneously, or scheduling overlaps create thematic competition.
AI and Critical Minerals courses bucked the trend, likely due to direct relevance to learners' work.
A review will be conducted after three months to assess the situation.
