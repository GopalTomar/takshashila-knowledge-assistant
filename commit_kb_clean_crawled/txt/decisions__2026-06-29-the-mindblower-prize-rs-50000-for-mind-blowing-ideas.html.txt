Context
Takshashila Institution regularly hosts 'tankscrub,' an internal event where ideas and research are shared and critiqued. The Mindblower Prize is an incentive introduced by Nitin Pai (likely the head of Takshashila) to encourage creative and original thinking among staff at all levels, rewarding intellectual boldness rather than fully polished work.
