AI's Impact on Public Discourse and Policy Analysis Quality
insights
AI tools are flooding discourse with both high-quality analysis and sophisticated-sounding bullshit, forcing policy institutions to rethink how they create distinctive, valuable content.
Published
March 16, 2026
TL;DR
: AI tools are flooding discourse with both high-quality analysis and sophisticated-sounding bullshit, forcing policy institutions to rethink how they create distinctive, valuable content.
Key Points
AI enables previously poor writers to create convincing but potentially low-quality long-form content that exploits cognitive biases
Quality analysts can now flood markets with decent analysis, driving the marginal price of such work toward zero
Organizations must simultaneously produce better analysis, differentiate themselves, and capture target audience attention
The information environment has become both more homogeneous and more polarized due to AI integration
Traditional expertise risks being drowned out by AI-generated content that appears sophisticated but lacks substance
Context
This conversation emerged from an operations meeting discussion about AI's transformative impact on policy discourse. The leadership team is grappling with how Takshashila can maintain relevance and influence in an environment where AI tools have dramatically lowered barriers to content creation while simultaneously flooding the market with analysis.
