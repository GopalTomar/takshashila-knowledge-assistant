AI's Impact on Policy Discourse: Quality Degradation and Competition
insights
AI has flooded policy discourse with low-quality content while reducing the value of good analysis, forcing think tanks to rethink how they stand out.
Published
April 14, 2026
TL;DR
: AI has flooded policy discourse with low-quality content while reducing the value of good analysis, forcing think tanks to rethink how they stand out.
Key Points
AI allows previously poor writers to create persuasive but low-quality content that exploits cognitive biases
Good analysts can now flood the market with decent analysis, driving the marginal value of quality work toward zero
The signal-to-noise ratio in policy discourse has reached an all-time low due to AI-generated content
Think tanks must solve three simultaneous challenges: produce better analysis, stand out from competitors, and capture target audience attention
The threshold for entering subject matter discussions has been eliminated by AI tools
Context
This discussion was prompted by observations about AI's transformative impact on the policy and analysis landscape. The conversation emerged from an operations meeting discussion about how Takshashila Institution should adapt to these changes in the information environment, where AI tools are fundamentally altering both the quality and quantity of policy discourse.
