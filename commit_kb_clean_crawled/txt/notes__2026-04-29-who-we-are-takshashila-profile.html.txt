TL;DR
: Takshashila is an independent think tank and graduate school of public policy based in Bengaluru, founded in 2010, working at the intersection of research, education, and public discourse.
Key Points
Independent think tank and graduate school of public policy, Bengaluru, est. 2010
Works on international relations, defence, national security, and technology policy
Three pillars: research, education (Policy School), and public discourse (podcast, newsletters, journal)
Engages with PMO, Ministry of Defence, and international policy dialogues
Alumni networks across multiple continents
Research Circles
Seven active research programmes:
Indo-Pacific Studies
High-Tech Geopolitics
Advanced Biology
Economic Policy
Strategic Studies
Advanced Military Technologies
Geospatial Research
Policy School
Founded 2012. Unique model: students study while maintaining employment. Flagship offering is the Graduate Certificate in Public Policy (GCPP) — 12 weeks, three specialisations:
Defence & Foreign Affairs
Tech & Policy
Advanced Public Policy
Also offers a year-long Post Graduate Programme (PGP) and shorter specialised courses.
