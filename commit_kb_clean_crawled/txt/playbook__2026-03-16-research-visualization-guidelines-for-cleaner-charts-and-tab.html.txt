TL;DR
: Takshashila researchers should follow data visualization best practices to improve the aesthetic quality and clarity of charts in publications.
Key Points
Delete grid lines from charts for cleaner appearance
Use clear titles that state the insight and include measurement units
Prefer bar/line/column charts over pie charts
Avoid 3D formatting - stick to simple 2D visualizations
