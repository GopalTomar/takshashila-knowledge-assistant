Key Points
Primary byline format: '[Name] is a/the [Designation] at the [Programme/Specialisation] at the Takshashila Institution. Views expressed in this article do not represent those of the institution.'
Shortened format for length-constrained publications: '[Name] is a/the [Designation] at the Takshashila Institution. Views expressed in this article do not represent those of the institution.'
All researchers must follow this standardized format when submitting op-eds to external publications
