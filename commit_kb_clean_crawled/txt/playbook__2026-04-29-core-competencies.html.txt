IGCSR Framework (for events)
When planning any event, map out: -
I
nterests — whose interests are at play? -
G
oals — what are we trying to achieve? -
C
ontrol — what can we control vs. not? -
S
takeholders — who matters and how? -
R
isks — what could go wrong?
MBM Planning
Minute-by-Minute: plan every stage of an event in time blocks. No segment should be unplanned.
Additional Responsibilities
Beyond the five competencies, all staff are expected to:
Produce blog content regularly
Know what all researchers and programmes are working on
Know all educational programmes offered
Identify key external stakeholders
Maintain a public-facing Twitter/X and LinkedIn presence aligned with their professional role
Accept one administrative duty
Maintain full accountability for assigned tasks
Embrace the principle:
"All of us do everything"
