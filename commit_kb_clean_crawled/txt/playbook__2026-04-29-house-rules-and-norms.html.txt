House Rules & Problem-Solving Norms
playbook
Day-to-day norms for working at Takshashila — from office conduct to how to raise problems.
Published
April 29, 2026
TL;DR
: Don't come with a problem — come with three alternatives and your preferred solution. Ask for forgiveness rather than permission. Exceed deadlines, delight stakeholders.
Key Points
Initiative is expected — if you see something inadequate, intervene
Small organisation means everyone does everything — no task is beneath anyone
Understand why a process exists before proposing to change it
Google first, then ask colleagues
Problem-Solving Norms
Three alternatives rule
: Never raise a problem without at least three possible solutions and your recommended one
Ask for forgiveness, not permission
: Act on good judgement; don't delay waiting for approval
Exceed and delight
: Deliver beyond what was asked, before it was due
When Given a Task, Clarify
Why? (purpose)
By when? (deadline)
Priority? (relative to other work)
What resources? (budget, people, tools)
