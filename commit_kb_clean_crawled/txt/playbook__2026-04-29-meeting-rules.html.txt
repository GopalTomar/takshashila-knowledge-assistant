Meeting Rules & Mandatory Meetings
playbook
Takshashila's meeting norms, the two mandatory all-staff meetings, and how to run events on time.
Published
April 29, 2026
TL;DR
: All meetings start and end on time without exception. Two mandatory all-staff meetings every week: Ops (Tuesday 11:30am) and Tankscrub (Thursday 9:30am).
Key Points
Punctuality is non-negotiable — inform in advance if you cannot attend
Events are classified so people know which ones require their presence
The flag system (Gold/Red/Yellow/White) is the standard meeting language
Reverse Bollywood Format: lead with your conclusion in 60 seconds, explain methodology after

The Reverse Bollywood Format
For any update or presentation:
Lead with the conclusion
— state your key point or recommendation in 60 seconds
Then explain
the methodology, evidence, or context
Do not build up to a conclusion. State it first.
After Conferences
Within one week of attending any external event, share learnings via email covering:
Content insights (max 3 points)
Organisational takeaways (max 3 points)
Collaboration opportunities (max 3 points)
