Your First Week at Takshashila — Checklist
playbook
What to do and expect in your first week as a new Takshashila staff member.
Published
April 29, 2026
TL;DR
: Expect chaos, embrace it. In week one: introduce yourself, get your email and tools set up, join Mattermost and Signal, add the calendars, sort health insurance.
What to Expect
New staff should expect "an incredible amount of chaos" with everyone performing multiple duties. This is normal. The organisation is flat and fast-moving. Your job is to get oriented quickly and start contributing.
Week One Checklist
Introduce yourself to all team members
Complete the staff information form
Obtain institutional email (
@takshashila.org.in
)
Join Mattermost (internal communications)
Join Signal (team channel)
Join OpenTakshashila (community platform)
Add the Takshashila Events calendar to your Google Calendar
Colour-code calendar entries by your research circle
Enrol in health insurance
Submit the Soma18 weekly form (Mondays, 6pm) — first submission due end of week one
