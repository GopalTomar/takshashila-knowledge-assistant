[2024-09-16 08:22 UTC] @nitinpai: 👉🏽 *Jump into conversations*

A lot of what I've learnt is by participating in conversations with my teachers, bosses, colleagues, customers, students, spouse & children. 

I notice many are shy or uncomfortable joining conversations in the office. Don't be. If there is a conversation happening in the main area (our deewan-e-aam) or the cafeteria, feel free to join in. 

By participating I mean both listening and contributing. You don't have to weigh in deeply, you can ask simple questions asking people to explain. But avoid being a passenger. 

PS. Our office is designed the way it is to facilitate this. A think tank is primarily a place for good quality conversations.
