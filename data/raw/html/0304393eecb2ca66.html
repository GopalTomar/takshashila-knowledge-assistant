Takshashila Executive Programme

Bespoke Courses

The Takshashila Executive Programme offers custom-designed courses for organisations seeking to build public policy capacity among their teams. These bespoke programmes are tailored to your organisation’s specific needs, combining Takshashila’s expertise in policy analysis, strategic thinking, and communication with your sector’s unique challenges.

Quick facts

FORMAT Custom
DURATION Flexible
DELIVERY Online · in-person · hybrid
CONTACT carl@takshashila.org.in

Who this is for

Corporations and Industry Bodies

Organisations seeking to equip their leadership and teams with public policy frameworks for navigating regulatory environments and government engagement.

Government and Public Sector

Government departments and public sector organisations looking to strengthen policy analysis, communication, and strategic thinking capabilities.

Diplomatic and Defence Establishments

Foreign affairs and defence organisations seeking specialised programmes on geopolitics, strategic studies, and international relations.

Media and Civil Society

Media organisations and NGOs seeking to build policy literacy and analytical capabilities within their teams.

How it works

Custom Curriculum

Programmes designed around your organisation's specific policy learning needs.

Flexible Format

Online, in-person, or hybrid delivery to suit your team's schedule.

Expert Faculty

Access to Takshashila's faculty and network of policy practitioners.

Policy Simulations

Hands-on exercises and simulations tailored to your sector.

Flexible Duration

From intensive workshops to multi-week programmes.

Certificate

Takshashila Executive Programme certificate for participants.
